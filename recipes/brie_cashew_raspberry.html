Brie cheese, cashew and raspberries pizza

Description of the recipe

This is a recipe for a pizza from a course that I attended

Ingredients

  • 2 pizza circles(pre-baked)
  • 100 ml of tomato sauce
  • 400 grams of mozzarella cheese
  • 300 grams of brie cheese
  • 200 grams of cashew nuts
  • 100 grams of raspberries or raspberry jam

Steps

  1. Add the tomato sauce to the pizza dough
  2. Add the cheese(both) and the cashew nuts
  3. Pre-heat the oven at 200Â°C
  4. Put it in the oven for 4 to 5 minutes
  5. remove from the oven and add the raspberries or jam
