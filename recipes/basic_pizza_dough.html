Pizza dough recipe

Description of the recipe

This is a recipe for a simple pizza dough from a course that I attended

Ingredients

  • 2 kilograms of wheat flour
  • 100 grams of sugar
  • 1 Liter of water
  • 50 grams of yeast
  • 40 mililiters of olive oil
  • 40 grams of salt

Steps

  1. Combine the flour,the sugar and the yeast in a bowl and stir
  2. Then put the water and keep stirring
  3. Add the salt and the oil and work the dough util it gets homogeneus
  4. Divide the dough in equal parts and knead
  5. Let it rest
  6. Roll the dough into circles and let it ferment
  7. Bake it at 130 Â°C for 15 minutes
