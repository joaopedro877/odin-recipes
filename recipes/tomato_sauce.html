Tomato sauce recipe

Description of the recipe

This is a recipe for a tomato sauce from a course that I attended

Ingredients

  • 2 kilograms of ripe tomatoes
  • 200 grams of tomato paste
  • 10 grams of garlic
  • 100 grams of chopped onions
  • 20 grams of salt

Steps

  1. Heat a pan
  2. Add garlic, onions and saute
  3. Add the tomatoes, the tomato paste and the salt
  4. Let it cook
  5. After cooked, wait until it cools and put it on a blender
